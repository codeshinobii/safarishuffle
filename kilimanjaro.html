Climb Preparation

Begin training 3-6 months before your climb:

  • Cardio 4-5 times/week (hiking, stair climbing, running)
  • Strength training for legs and core
  • Practice hiking with a weighted backpack
  • Altitude training if possible

Essential gear for your climb:

  • 4-season sleeping bag
  • Insulated hiking boots
  • Moisture-wicking layers
  • Down jacket
  • Headlamp with extra batteries
  • Trekking poles
  • Sunglasses (UV400)
  • Water purification tablets

  • Choose a longer route for better acclimatization
  • Drink 4-5 liters of water daily
  • Climb high, sleep low
  • Recognize AMS symptoms
  • Consider Diamox after consulting your doctor
