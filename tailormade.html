Our Planning Process

1

Share Your Vision

Tell us about your dream safari - your interests, travel dates, group size, budget, and any special requirements. This can be through our online form, email, or a phone consultation with our safari specialist.

2

Expert Consultation

We'll assign you a personal safari planner who will contact you to discuss options, make recommendations based on your preferences, and answer all your questions about Tanzania's parks and seasons.

3

Custom Itinerary Draft

Within 48 hours, we'll send you a detailed proposed itinerary with lodge options, activities, and pricing. We'll refine this together until every detail matches your expectations.

4

Confirm & Prepare

Once you're happy with your itinerary, we'll handle all bookings and provide comprehensive pre-travel information. Your personal safari planner remains available for any questions right up to departure.

5

Enjoy Your Safari

Experience Tanzania exactly as you envisioned, with our local team available 24/7 during your trip to ensure everything runs smoothly.
