Tour Overview

Experience the greatest wildlife spectacle on earth - the annual Great Migration in Serengeti National Park. This 5-day safari follows the massive herds of wildebeest and zebra as they traverse the plains, with expert guides to maximize your wildlife viewing opportunities.

Witness dramatic river crossings, predator action, and the breathtaking landscapes of the Serengeti. Our carefully designed itinerary ensures you'll be in the best locations at the optimal times for migration viewing.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport, where you'll be met by our representative and transferred to your lodge in Arusha. Evening briefing about your upcoming safari.

Accommodation: Arusha Coffee Lodge

Day 2: Arusha to Central Serengeti

Morning flight to Serengeti. Afternoon game drive in Seronera Valley, known for its year-round wildlife concentrations and scenic kopjes.

Accommodation: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge

Day 3: Full Day Migration Viewing

Full day dedicated to following the migration herds. Picnic lunch in the bush. Depending on season, witness river crossings or calving grounds.

Accommodation: Serengeti Serena Safari Lodge

Day 4: Northern Serengeti

Transfer to northern Serengeti (seasonal). Game drives focusing on the Mara River crossings (July-October) or western corridor (May-July).

Accommodation: Sayari Camp

Day 5: Departure

Morning game drive followed by transfer to airstrip for flight back to Arusha. Connect with your international flight or extend your stay.
