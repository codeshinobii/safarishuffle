Travel Planning

Best Time to Visit

Zanzibar enjoys warm tropical weather year-round, but certain periods offer ideal conditions:

Dry Season (June-October)

Cooler temperatures (25-28°C) with low humidity. Perfect for beach activities and cultural tours.

Short Rains (November-December)

Brief showers but still plenty of sunshine. Fewer crowds and lower prices.

Hot Season (January-February)

Hottest months (28-32°C) but excellent for water activities. Great for kitesurfing.

Long Rains (March-May)

Heavier rainfall, especially April. Some resorts close, but significant discounts available.
