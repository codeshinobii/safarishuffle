Zanzibar Experiences

From spice tours to pristine beaches, discover the many faces of this Indian Ocean gem

Beach Bliss

Pristine white sand beaches with turquoise waters. Top spots include Nungwi, Kendwa, and Paje for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

  • Powder-soft white sand
  • Kite surfing & diving
  • Sunset dhow cruises

Cultural Heritage

Explore Stone Town's labyrinthine alleys, a UNESCO site blending Swahili, Arab, Persian, Indian and European influences.

  • Historic House of Wonders
  • Old Fort & Sultan's Palace
  • Freddie Mercury Museum

Spice Tours

Discover why Zanzibar is called the "Spice Island" with tours of working plantations growing cloves, vanilla, cinnamon and nutmeg.

  • Taste fresh tropical fruits
  • Learn traditional uses
  • Spice market visits

Island Highlights

Marine Wonders

Snorkel with tropical fish at Mnemba Atoll or swim with dolphins off Kizimkazi.

Food Paradise

Sample Zanzibar pizza, seafood curries, and the famous urojo soup at Forodhani Gardens night market.

Historical Sites

Visit the slave market memorial and ancient Persian baths at Kidichi.
